Meet Svitlana

"I have always been inspired by moments of genuine, human emotion."

For many years, Svitlana carried one idea — to create a place where real emotions could live and be shared. Soniashnyk is that place.

Meaning sunflower in Ukrainian, the name reflects a journey from finance and sociology to the delicate art of confectionery — always observing, learning, and collecting emotions along the way.

"Every sweet is a small moment of warmth that people can pass on to one another."

Svitlana, Founder

Rooted in tradition. Crafted for connection.
